Devices, Devices, Devices

Wireless devices like smartphones, tablets and IoT devices are exploding globally. The changes that are taking place in networking puts the device at the centre for consumer and enterprise connectivity. With the “Cloud” as the endpoint from where services are rendered, analysed and optimised. Device manufacturers, mobile operators and others in the wireless ecosystem have no choice but to optimise their products and services to provide quality customer experience.

Measure & Analyze, Anywhere, Anytime

Airometric system provides a cloud based solution to measure, manage and analyze the performance of wireless devices and networks. Data can be captured anywhere globally. Our web portal, again accessible from anywhere with an internet connection, provides you the ability to manage the process and gives you visibility into performance data collected during tests within a short period of time after test completion.

Real World Data

Airometric’s solution allows any user in any global location to do quick voice call connectivity and data performance tests on the actual network. Advanced analytics and reporting even allows comparative analysis of different tests on different devices in different locations. Our web portal provides a unified view, allowing you to analyze and optimize voice call and data performance from a device and/or network perspective to ensure optimum customer experience.

Connectivity & Performance

Airometric System provides logs and contextual information from the application layer on the mobile device. Test voice connectivity and data performance using just your unmodified device so you know what your customer experiences.

Visualize Network Performance Instantly

Using the Airometric applications on any unmodified device, instantly assess performance during network installation, changes and network issue resolution. No need for laptops and complex device setups.

Cloud-based Rich Reporting

Performance data collected by Airometric applications are processed by the cloud-based Airometrics Analytics Engine to produce feature-rich reports that are instantly accessible globally.
